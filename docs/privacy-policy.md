## Images and Skin Scans

**[DRAFT — legal review required]**

- Skin images are analyzed **on-device** whenever technically possible. We do not upload your skin images to our servers without your **explicit, informed consent**.
- If a future feature requires server-side processing, you will be clearly informed and asked to opt in before any image leaves your device.
- Images used for on-device scans are not retained beyond the active session unless you explicitly choose to save them to your personal history.
