# Medical Disclaimer

Shyama is an **educational tool** designed to help you learn about common skin conditions. It is **not a medical device and does not provide a medical diagnosis.**

The information provided is for general educational purposes only. Skin conditions can look very different depending on your skin tone — and our model has real limitations. It may not accurately reflect how conditions appear on all skin tones.

**Always consult a licensed dermatologist** before making any decisions about your skin health, especially if you notice:

- Changes in the size, shape, or color of a mole
- New or unusual growths
- Anything that bleeds, itches persistently, or won't heal

**If you experience sudden swelling, severe pain, spreading redness with fever, or any other emergency — seek urgent medical care immediately.**

*Shyama is a starting point for curiosity, not a substitute for professional care.*
